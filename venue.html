AICAPS 2026

The 2nd International Conference on Advances in Intelligent Computing and Applications (AICAPS) 2026 aims to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange among researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and domain experts. Centered around the theme of Advancements in Intelligent Computing, the event will explore a wide spectrum, from foundational theories to practical implementations, encompassing case studies, ongoing research, and conceptual innovations. The conference aspires to broaden the research outlook by highlighting real-world applications and emerging challenges in the field.
