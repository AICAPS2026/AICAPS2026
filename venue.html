Department of Computer Applications, CUSAT

The Department of Computer Applications, established in 1994 at CUSAT, offers AICTE-approved programs including MCA, Integrated MCA, and Executive M.Tech, along with M.Sc in Computer Science with specialization in AI and M.Sc in Computer Science with specialization in Data Science and Ph.D. in Technology. Supported by experienced faculty with expertise in AI, data science, blockchain, machine learning, and cybersecurity, the department has published over 150 research papers and secured projects worth over ₹1 crore. It combines strong academic foundations with hands-on training and regularly conducts workshops, seminars, Faculty Development Programs (FDPs), Short Term Training Programs (STTPs), and conferences to address real-world industrial and societal challenges.
