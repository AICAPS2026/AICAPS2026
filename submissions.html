Call for Papers

The 2nd International Conference on Advances in Intelligent Computing and Applications (AICAPS) 2026 aims to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange among researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and domain experts. Centered around the theme of Advancements in Intelligent Computing, the event will explore a wide spectrum, from foundational theories to practical implementations, encompassing case studies, ongoing research, and conceptual innovations. The conference aspires to broaden the research outlook by highlighting real-world applications and emerging challenges in the field. It offers an excellent platform for participants to engage with leading experts, gain insights into cutting-edge developments, and explore new directions for impactful research.

Conference Tracks

  • Smart Computing and Applications
  • Communication and Signal Processing
  • Technological Systems for a Secure and Sustainable World
  • Intelligent Systems and Analytics
  • Smart Embedded Systems

Submission Guidelines

  • Submissions should consist of a PDF with no more than 6 double-column pages, excluding references and appendices (max 2 pages). The total PDF must not exceed 8 pages.
  • Paper Format: Use the IEEE template for your paper.
  • Anonymity: Submissions must be anonymous. Author names and affiliations should not be included.
